## 1. The Problem & Opportunity

Small and medium businesses (SMBs) are increasingly targeted by sophisticated cyber threats but lack access to affordable, effective, and easy-to-manage endpoint security solutions. Existing products are often too complex, expensive, or not tailored to SMB needs, leaving them exposed to malware, ransomware, and data breaches. The Indian SMB cybersecurity market is rapidly growing, presenting a significant opportunity for innovation.
